How Do Storms Develop and Intensify?

This interactive visualization uses GOES-16 satellite imagery to explore how cloud-top temperature changes as Hurricane Laura developed over time.

GOES C13 Cloud-Top Temperature

Brighter areas represent colder cloud tops, which often indicate taller and more intense storm clouds.

Storm Intensity Proxy Over Time

The intensity proxy is based on cold cloud coverage and minimum cloud-top temperature.
